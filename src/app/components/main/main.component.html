Whats wrong

How it works?

  • Anyone from the community can post an issue
  • People can discuss an issue
  • People can vote for issues they find important, that's how an issue is chosen as the most critical at the moment

Functionality

  • Sign up and complete your profile
  • Post an issue
  • You can vote for others' issues
  • Anyone of our community can vote for your issue
  • Anyone of our community can extend your issue
  • You can vote for any issue
  • You can extend any issue
  • Issues owner can accept or decline extension to his issue
